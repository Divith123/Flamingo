# Flamingo Invoice - Simple Invoicing and Payment Requests

## Overview

Flamingo Invoice is the streamlined invoicing and payment request module within the Flamingo financial ecosystem. Designed for freelancers, small businesses, and service-based organizations, it transforms the traditionally cumbersome billing process into an elegant, efficient workflow. The module enables users to create professional invoices, send payment requests, track receivables, and collect payments—all from a single, intuitive interface.

The core value proposition centers on simplicity without sacrificing power. Freelancers can create and send their first invoice in under two minutes, while growing businesses benefit from advanced features like recurring billing, automated reminders, multi-currency support, and comprehensive reporting. Every invoice automatically integrates with Flamingo Books for accounts receivable tracking, Flamingo Payments for payment processing, and Flamingo Sign when contracts require electronic signatures.

Flamingo Invoice targets independent professionals, creative agencies, consulting firms, and small service businesses that need professional billing capabilities without enterprise complexity. The module supports the entire invoice lifecycle from initial quote through final payment reconciliation, including estimates that convert to invoices, partial payment handling, and automated late fee calculations.

The three pillars of Flamingo Invoice are: speed (create and send invoices in minutes, not hours), professionalism (customizable branding that reflects your business identity), and cash flow optimization (automated reminders, multiple payment options, and real-time payment tracking). By integrating seamlessly with the broader Flamingo ecosystem, Invoice eliminates double data entry and provides a unified view of customer relationships and financial performance.

## Market Analysis

### Market Landscape

The invoicing software market represents a significant segment of the broader financial technology ecosystem, with estimates projecting continued growth as more businesses digitize their billing processes. The market spans from simple tools targeting solo freelancers to comprehensive billing platforms serving enterprise organizations with complex subscription models and global operations.

Key market trends shaping product development include:

- **Embedded payments**: The shift from invoice-as-document to invoice-as-payment-experience, where clients can pay directly from the invoice view without navigating to separate payment portals.
- **Automation at scale**: AI-powered features including smart line item suggestions, automatic tax calculation, predictive payment dates, and intelligent reminder timing based on client payment history.
- **Mobile-first creation**: Over 40% of invoices are now created on mobile devices, requiring responsive design and native mobile capabilities for on-the-go billing.
- **Global commerce support**: Multi-currency invoicing, localized tax compliance, and international payment methods are increasingly essential as businesses serve global client bases.
- **Subscription billing convergence**: The blurring lines between traditional invoicing and subscription billing, with businesses expecting support for both one-time and recurring revenue models.

## Core Features

### 1. Invoice Creation

The invoice creation system prioritizes speed and flexibility while maintaining professional output quality:

- **Smart templates**: Choose from professionally designed templates optimized for different industries (professional services, creative agencies, consulting, retail). Templates automatically adapt to content length and complexity while maintaining visual consistency.

- **Intelligent line items**: The line item editor supports multiple types including standard items, time-based billing with rate and hours, expense reimbursement with markup percentages, subscription items with proration, and discount line items. Auto-complete suggests previously used items with historical pricing.

- **Flexible tax handling**: Configure single or multiple tax rates, compound taxes, tax-inclusive or tax-exclusive pricing, and per-line-item or invoice-level tax application. Tax rates can be associated with specific products, services, or client locations for automatic application.

- **Discount strategies**: Apply percentage or fixed-amount discounts at the line item or invoice level. Configure early payment discounts (e.g., "2/10 Net 30") with automatic calculation and tracking. Volume discount tables can be predefined for specific clients or product categories.

- **Payment terms**: Define standard payment terms (Due on Receipt, Net 15, Net 30, Net 60) with custom date calculations. Configure deposit requirements with percentage or fixed amounts due upfront. Payment plans allow splitting invoice totals across multiple scheduled payments.

- **File attachments**: Attach supporting documents to invoices including contracts, delivery receipts, timesheet details, or expense receipts. Attached files are included in PDF generation and accessible through the client portal.

### 2. Customization

Comprehensive branding and customization ensure every invoice reflects your business identity:

- **Brand identity**: Upload your business logo in multiple formats (PNG, SVG, JPG) with automatic sizing and positioning. Configure primary and secondary brand colors that apply to headers, accents, and action buttons. Select from curated font pairings or specify custom web fonts.

- **Template customization**: Modify template layouts including header positioning, footer content, column widths, and section ordering. Add custom fields to invoice headers and line items for industry-specific information. Configure which sections appear (item descriptions, quantities, rates, totals, tax breakdowns).

- **Document configuration**: Customize invoice numbering schemes with prefixes, suffixes, and sequential or date-based numbering. Configure default notes and terms that appear on all invoices with per-invoice override capability. Set up multiple invoice templates for different business lines or client types.

- **Email personalization**: Customize email templates for invoice delivery, payment receipts, payment reminders, and thank-you messages. Use merge fields for dynamic content including client name, invoice amount, due date, and payment link. Configure sender name and reply-to addresses.

- **Custom fields**: Create custom fields at the invoice level (purchase order number, project code, department) and line item level (SKU, cost center, delivery date). Custom fields can be configured as required or optional, with validation rules and default values.

- **Multi-language support**: Generate invoices in multiple languages with localized date formats, number formatting, and currency symbols. Email templates support multiple languages with automatic language selection based on client preferences.

### 3. Delivery

Multiple delivery channels ensure invoices reach clients through their preferred methods:

- **Email delivery**: Send invoices directly from the platform with customizable email templates, attachment options (PDF included or linked), and delivery tracking. Email scheduling allows sending at optimal times for client engagement. Bounce detection and resend workflows handle delivery failures.

- **PDF generation**: Generate high-quality PDF invoices optimized for both screen viewing and printing. PDFs include embedded fonts, proper pagination for multi-page invoices, and optional password protection. Batch PDF generation supports printing or archiving multiple invoices.

- **Shareable links**: Generate secure, unique URLs for each invoice that can be shared via messaging apps, SMS, or embedded in client portals. Links can be configured with expiration dates, password protection, and access logging. QR codes on PDFs link directly to online payment pages.

- **Client portal**: Provide clients with a self-service portal where they can view all invoices, download PDFs, make payments, and view payment history. Portal access is controlled through secure login or magic link authentication. Brand the portal with your logo and colors.

- **API delivery**: Programmatically create and send invoices through the REST API for integration with CRM, project management, or e-commerce systems. Webhooks notify external systems of invoice status changes (sent, viewed, paid, overdue).

- **Snail mail**: Integration with postal mail services enables printing, stuffing, and mailing physical invoices for clients who require paper documentation. Return envelope inclusion and certified mail options are available.

### 4. Payment Collection

Comprehensive payment options maximize collection rates and client convenience:

- **Online payments**: Accept credit and debit cards through integrated payment processing with PCI-compliant security. Support for Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and international card networks. One-click payment for returning customers with saved payment methods.

- **Bank transfers**: Accept ACH bank transfers with lower transaction fees than card payments. Support for wire transfers for larger invoices with automatic reconciliation. Instant bank verification through micro-deposit or instant account verification services.

- **Digital wallets**: Accept payments through Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, and other popular digital wallets. Mobile-optimized checkout flows maximize conversion on smartphones and tablets.

- **Partial payments**: Allow clients to make partial payments against invoice balances with automatic remaining balance calculation. Configure minimum partial payment amounts and payment plan structures. Split payments across multiple payment methods.

- **Deposits and retainers**: Require deposits or retainer payments before work begins with automatic credit application to final invoices. Track retainer balances across multiple invoices with automatic replenishment reminders.

- **Payment plans**: Convert large invoices into structured payment plans with scheduled installments. Automatic payment processing on scheduled dates with reminder notifications. Late payment fees can be configured for missed installment payments.

- **Offline payments**: Record cash, check, and other offline payment methods with deposit tracking and check number recording. Offline payments trigger the same automated workflows (receipts, thank-you messages) as online payments.

### 5. Recurring Invoices

Automated recurring billing streamlines subscription and retainer-based business models:

- **Schedule configuration**: Set up recurring invoices with daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annual intervals. Configure specific send dates (e.g., "1st of every month") or relative dates (e.g., "30 days after last invoice"). Proration handling for mid-cycle start dates.

- **Auto-send or draft**: Choose between automatic sending upon generation or draft creation for review before sending. Review queues show upcoming recurring invoices with options to edit, skip, or cancel individual occurrences.

- **Variable line items**: Configure recurring invoices with fixed line items that repeat every cycle or variable line items populated from usage data, time tracking, or external integrations. Smart suggestions based on historical patterns.

- **End conditions**: Define end dates, maximum invoice counts, or ongoing until manually cancelled. Automatic cancellation upon client payment default or contract termination.

- **Billing integration**: Deep integration with Flamingo Billing for complex subscription management including tiered pricing, usage-based billing, and automatic plan changes. Subscription changes automatically update recurring invoice templates.

- **Dunning management**: Automated workflows for failed payments including retry schedules, payment method update requests, and service suspension triggers. Dunning email sequences with configurable timing and messaging.

### 6. Estimates and Quotes

Professional quoting capabilities that seamlessly convert to invoices:

- **Estimate creation**: Create detailed estimates and quotes using the same interface as invoices with modified styling to distinguish quotes from final invoices. Include optional line items with client-selectable choices and package deals.

- **Approval workflow**: Send estimates to clients for electronic approval through the client portal or email. Approval triggers automatic conversion to an active invoice with preserved line items and pricing. Rejection options with reason capture.

- **Version control**: Create multiple versions of estimates as scope changes with automatic numbering (Revision 1, Revision 2). Version comparison shows differences between estimate versions. Client approval of specific versions with automatic archival of outdated versions.

- **Expiration dates**: Set automatic expiration dates for estimates with configurable extension options. Expired estimates can be converted to inactive status or automatically followed up with renewal offers.

- **One-click conversion**: Convert approved estimates to invoices with a single click, preserving all line items, custom fields, and attachments. Option to copy or link the original estimate for reference. Automatic notification to clients upon conversion.

- **Quote analytics**: Track estimate acceptance rates, average time to approval, and revenue in pipeline. Identify frequently rejected line items or pricing tiers for optimization.

### 7. Expense Billing

Capture and bill client-related expenses with markup and documentation:

- **Expense capture**: Record billable expenses with receipt attachment, vendor information, and expense categorization. Mobile receipt capture with OCR extraction of amount, date, and merchant. Bulk expense import from credit card feeds or CSV files.

- **Markup configuration**: Apply configurable markup percentages to reimbursable expenses (e.g., 10% markup on travel expenses, 15% on materials). Markup can be displayed as separate line items or included in rolled-up amounts.

- **Expense attachments**: Original receipts can be attached to client invoices as supporting documentation. Receipt images are optimized for viewing and printing with automatic rotation and cropping.

- **Approval workflow**: Billable expenses may require manager or project lead approval before inclusion on client invoices. Approval chains ensure proper cost allocation and prevent unauthorized billing.

- **Pass-through billing**: Pass expenses through at cost without markup for transparency-focused client relationships. Detailed expense reports accompany invoices showing exact costs incurred.

- **Integration with Expense module**: Direct integration with Flamingo Expense for seamless flow of approved expenses into the invoicing workflow. Expense categorization in Expense maps to invoice line item categories.

### 8. Client Management

Comprehensive customer directory with relationship insights:

- **Client profiles**: Maintain detailed client records including contact information, billing addresses, shipping addresses, tax IDs, and payment terms. Multiple contact persons per client with role-based notification routing.

- **Payment history**: Complete invoice and payment history for each client with aging summaries and average payment time calculations. Payment pattern analysis identifies consistently late-paying clients.

- **Credit limits**: Configure credit limits with automatic hold triggers when outstanding balances exceed thresholds. Credit limit utilization visible during invoice creation.

- **Client groups**: Organize clients into groups for bulk operations, custom pricing, and reporting. Groups can represent regions, industries, account tiers, or sales territories.

- **Communication log**: Track all client communications including sent invoices, payment reminders, payment receipts, and manual notes. Email open tracking shows when clients have viewed invoices.

- **Import and export**: Bulk import client lists from CSV files with field mapping. Export client data for backup or integration with CRM systems.

### 9. Automated Reminders

Intelligent payment reminder system that reduces manual follow-up:

- **Reminder sequences**: Configure multi-stage reminder sequences (e.g., 7 days before due, on due date, 7 days after due, 14 days after due). Each stage can have different messaging tone and delivery channel.

- **Smart timing**: Machine learning optimizes reminder send times based on client email open patterns and payment behavior. Weekend and holiday detection prevents inappropriate reminder timing.

- **Custom messaging**: Customize reminder email templates for different stages with progressively urgent language. Include payment links, invoice PDFs, and account manager contact information.

- **Late fees**: Automatically calculate and apply late fees based on configurable rules (percentage of balance, fixed amount, or tiered structure). Late fee line items added to overdue invoices with clear disclosure.

- **Escalation workflows**: Escalate severely overdue accounts to collections workflow or account manager notification. Integration with Flamingo CRM for sales team visibility into payment issues.

- **Pause and resume**: Pause reminder sequences for clients with payment arrangements or disputes. Manual reminder sending for exceptional situations.

### 10. Multi-Currency

Support global business operations with comprehensive currency handling:

- **Invoice in any currency**: Create invoices in 135+ currencies with localized formatting. Clients see invoices in their preferred currency while you track receivables in your base currency.

- **Exchange rate handling**: Real-time exchange rate feeds from major financial data providers with historical rate lookup. Exchange rates lock at invoice creation or can update until payment receipt.

- **Multi-currency reporting**: View accounts receivable and revenue reports in base currency with original currency amounts preserved. Currency gain/loss tracking for exchange rate fluctuations between invoice and payment dates.

- **Localized compliance**: Support for country-specific invoice requirements including VAT numbers, tax registration displays, and regulatory text. Configurable invoice footers for jurisdiction-specific legal language.

- **Payment currency matching**: Accept payments in invoice currency or allow currency conversion at point of payment. Multi-currency bank account reconciliation support.

### 11. Reporting and Analytics

Comprehensive insights into billing performance and receivables:

- **Aging reports**: Accounts receivable aging with configurable buckets (current, 1-30 days, 31-60 days, 61-90 days, 90+ days). Aging by client, by invoice date, or by due date. Drill-down from summary to individual invoices.

- **Revenue analysis**: Revenue by client, by time period, by product/service, and by sales territory. Trend analysis with year-over-year and month-over-month comparisons. Forecasting based on recurring revenue and pipeline estimates.

- **Payment performance**: Average days to payment by client and overall. Payment method analysis showing which methods have highest collection rates. Failed payment tracking and retry success rates.

- **Tax summaries**: Sales tax collected by jurisdiction with filing-ready reports. Tax liability reports organized by filing period. Support for multiple tax types (sales tax, VAT, GST).

- **Custom dashboards**: Build custom analytics dashboards with drag-and-drop widgets. Save and share dashboard configurations with team members. Schedule automated report delivery via email.

- **Data export**: Export all reports to PDF, Excel, or CSV formats. API access to reporting data for custom analytics and business intelligence integration.

### 12. Mobile Capabilities

Full-featured mobile experience for invoicing on the go:

- **Mobile invoice creation**: Create and send invoices from iOS and Android apps with streamlined interfaces optimized for touch input. Voice-to-text for line item descriptions. Camera integration for receipt capture.

- **Offline functionality**: Create drafts offline with automatic synchronization when connectivity returns. View cached invoice and client data without internet connection.

- **Push notifications**: Real-time notifications for payment receipt, invoice viewing, and overdue alerts. Customizable notification preferences by event type.

- **Mobile payments**: Accept in-person payments through mobile card readers with automatic invoice marking as paid. QR code generation for contactless payment acceptance.

- **Quick actions**: Widget and shortcut support for common actions (create invoice, record payment, view aging). Time tracking integration for service professionals.

- **Biometric security**: Face ID and fingerprint authentication for sensitive operations including payment processing and invoice sending.

## Integration Points

### Flamingo Payments

The integration with Flamingo Payments provides comprehensive payment processing:

- **Payment processing**: Invoices link directly to payment processing infrastructure supporting credit cards, ACH, digital wallets, and alternative payment methods. Payment buttons embedded in invoices and client portal enable one-click payment.

- **Automatic reconciliation**: Received payments automatically update invoice status, create payment records, and post to accounts receivable in Books. Real-time payment status synchronization keeps invoice and payment data consistent.

- **Failed payment handling**: Failed payments trigger automated dunning workflows, retry schedules, and client notifications. Failed payment reasons logged for troubleshooting.

- **Refund processing**: Refunds initiated from Invoice interface process through Payments module with automatic invoice balance adjustment and receipt generation.

- **Payment method management**: Client saved payment methods stored securely in Payments module with tokenization. Payment method update workflows handle expired cards and failed payment recovery.

### Flamingo Books

The integration with Flamingo Books ensures financial records stay synchronized:

- **AR posting**: Invoice creation automatically creates accounts receivable journal entries in Books, debiting AR and crediting revenue accounts. Invoice edits generate adjustment entries.

- **Payment posting**: Received payments create cash receipts journal entries, debiting cash and crediting AR. Payment allocation to specific invoices tracked for accurate AR aging.

- **Revenue recognition**: Revenue from invoices flows into Books revenue accounts with proper period recognition. Deferred revenue handling for prepayments and deposits.

- **Chart of accounts mapping**: Invoice line items map to revenue accounts in the Books chart of accounts. Product and service categories drive automatic account assignment.

- **Financial reporting**: Invoice data feeds into Books financial reports including income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements with drill-down to invoice detail.

### Flamingo Sign

Integration with Flamingo Sign enables contract and agreement workflows:

- **Contract attachment**: Signed contracts from Sign module attach to invoices for client reference and legal documentation. Contract execution triggers automatic invoice generation for milestone payments.

- **Signature requirements**: Estimates and engagement letters can require electronic signature approval before conversion to active invoices. Signature status visible in invoice workflow.

- **Document linking**: Invoices reference related signed documents (statements of work, master service agreements) with clickable links to document repository.

- **Audit trail**: Signed documents linked to invoices provide complete audit trail for contract compliance and dispute resolution.

### Flamingo Billing

The integration with Flamingo Billing handles complex subscription and recurring revenue:

- **Subscription invoicing**: Billing module subscription events automatically generate invoices through Invoice module with proper line items, proration, and tax calculation.

- **Usage billing**: Metered usage data from Billing flows into Invoice line items for usage-based billing scenarios.

- **Plan change handling**: Subscription upgrades, downgrades, and cancellations trigger invoice adjustments, credits, or final invoices through the Invoice module.

- **Dunning integration**: Billing dunning workflows leverage Invoice reminder capabilities with coordinated messaging and escalation.

- **Revenue recognition**: Invoice data feeds back to Billing for revenue recognition reporting and forecasting.

### Flamingo Expense

The integration with Flamingo Expense streamlines billable expense handling:

- **Expense import**: Approved expenses marked as billable in Expense module flow into Invoice module for client billing with receipt attachments and markup calculation.

- **Markup application**: Configurable markup percentages applied to imported expenses based on expense category and client agreements.

- **Reimbursement coordination**: Personal expenses billed to clients offset employee reimbursements, preventing double payment.

- **Cost allocation**: Expense cost centers and projects map to invoice line item dimensions for accurate profitability reporting.

### CRM Integration

Client relationship data synchronizes with Flamingo CRM:

- **Contact sync**: Client records created in Invoice sync to CRM as contacts and accounts with full communication history.

- **Opportunity linking**: CRM opportunities can generate estimates and invoices with automatic pipeline stage updates upon invoice payment.

- **Activity logging**: Invoice events (sent, viewed, paid, overdue) logged as CRM activities for complete customer interaction history.

- **Sales visibility**: Sales teams view invoice and payment status in CRM for customer health monitoring and upsell timing.

### Third-Party Integrations

- **Payment gateways**: Stripe, PayPal, Square, and other major payment processors integrated through Payments module.

- **Accounting software**: Export capabilities for QuickBooks, Xero, and other accounting platforms not using Flamingo Books.

- **Time tracking**: Integration with popular time tracking tools for time-based invoice line item population.

- **E-commerce**: Shopify, WooCommerce, and other e-commerce platforms can generate invoices for orders requiring billing.

## Technical Considerations

### PDF Generation

- **Rendering engine**: Server-side PDF generation using modern rendering libraries for consistent output across devices and platforms.

- **Template system**: Configurable templates using structured markup with variable substitution for dynamic content.

- **Performance**: PDF generation optimized for sub-2-second response time for standard invoices. Batch generation supported for high-volume scenarios.

- **Accessibility**: Generated PDFs include proper tagging and metadata for screen reader compatibility.

### Email Deliverability

- **Infrastructure**: Dedicated email infrastructure with proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC configuration for high deliverability rates.

- **Template engine**: Responsive email templates tested across major email clients with fallback for image blocking.

- **Tracking**: Open tracking and click tracking on invoice emails with privacy-compliant implementation.

- **Bounce handling**: Automatic bounce detection with invalid email flagging and sender notification.

### Payment Reconciliation

- **Transaction matching**: Automatic matching of bank deposits to invoice payments based on amount, reference numbers, and client information.

- **Exception handling**: Unmatched payments queued for manual review with suggested matches based on fuzzy matching algorithms.

- **Multi-currency handling**: Currency conversion at payment time with gain/loss calculation for exchange rate differences.

- **Audit trail**: Complete audit trail of payment processing from receipt through reconciliation with user attribution.

### Security

- **Data encryption**: Sensitive data encrypted at rest including client payment methods and bank account information.

- **Access control**: Role-based access control with granular permissions for invoice viewing, creation, editing, and payment processing.

- **PCI compliance**: Payment card data handling compliant with PCI DSS requirements through tokenization and secure vault storage.

- **Audit logging**: Immutable audit logs for all invoice and payment actions supporting compliance and dispute resolution.

### Performance

- **Scalability**: Architecture supports high invoice volumes with horizontal scaling capabilities for peak periods (month-end billing).

- **Caching**: Intelligent caching of client data, templates, and exchange rates for sub-second response times.

- **Background processing**: Email sending, PDF generation, and report generation handled asynchronously to maintain UI responsiveness.
